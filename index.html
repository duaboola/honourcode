Early Education Training Programme

Welcome to the Honour Code early education training programme. Honour Code focuses on nurturing young minds with the help of the Montessori method of education which is a type of educational method that involves children's natural interests and activities and develops critical thinking skills in early childhood. Honour Code programme of early childhood education covers both physical and psychological development to ensure a well-rounded approach. Integrating these elements, we aim to provide a solid foundation for young learners' future learning and growth.
